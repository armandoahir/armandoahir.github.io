Here is a list of my personal constraints.
<!-- more -->
Some of the items on this list are habits I’ve left behind over time. <br>
**Important**: This is not meant to promote any particular lifestyle, nor to suggest that my choices are better or worse than anyone else’s. It’s simply a personal reflection. If you find yourself feeling offended, this probably isn’t written for you.
I avoid habits that negatively affect my physical or mental well-being. I try to prioritize consistency over intensity in my routines.

### Media and entertainment
- I don’t use social media of any kind.
- I don't use mobile phone for stuff besides phone calls and messages.
- I don’t watch TV.
- Series > movies > cinema > theatre.
- I rarely watch anime. I don't read manga.

### Diet
- I don’t consume caffeine after 1 p.m.
- I don’t eat carbohydrates after 5 p.m.
- I don’t smoke.
- I don’t drink alcohol.
- I don’t drink soft drinks (except on occasional cheat days).
- I don’t eat horse meat.
- I don't eat shrimps, oysters and crabs.
- I don’t use margarine.

### Social life
- I don't go to church, i define myself as an agnostic.
- I don't like to discuss about my political compass.
- I don't like to discuss about LGBT+.
- I don't like if someone touches me.
- I prefer text messages over voice messages.
- I rarely use emojis and, if i use them, i don't like to link a particular meaning on them.
